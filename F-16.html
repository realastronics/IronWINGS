F-16 Fighting Falcon

Experience the legendary F-16 Fighting Falcon, the iconic multirole fighter that has dominated skies since 1978. Known as the "Viper," this single-engine supersonic jet combines agility, firepower, and advanced avionics, making it a must-have for any aviation enthusiast's collection. Our 1:72 scale model captures every detail of this air superiority masterpiece.
